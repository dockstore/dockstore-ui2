Register and publish your own tools and workflows

Dockstore uses the combination of containers and descriptor languages to make analysis portable and reproducible. Users can register their tools and workflows from a variety of third-party sites like Quay.io, DockerHub, GitHub, Bitbucket, and GitLab.
