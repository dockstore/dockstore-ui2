Create

Create reproducible workflows using popular technologies and standards.

Reproducible environment

The execution environment is described by Docker and built on platforms like Quay.io or Docker Hub.

Workflow descriptions

Workflows are described with CWL, WDL or Nextflow and stored on GitHub, Bitbucket, GitLab, or directly on Dockstore.

Share

Make your workflows more accessible to the scientific community.

Shareable

Our implementation of community standards from the GA4GH ensures your workflows are findable and interoperable.

Descriptive

Create a landing page for your workflow to share with the community.

Organizations

Create organizations for your lab, company, or university where you can have collections of workflows.

Use

Discover and launch scientific workflows locally and in the cloud.

Online launch

Select from a variety of launch partners including DNAstack, Terra, and DNAnexus.

Local launch

Use the Dockstore CLI to run workflows locally on Linux/Ubuntu and Mac OSX.
